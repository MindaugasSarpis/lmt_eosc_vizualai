## Project Overview

Data visualization project for the European Open Science Cloud (EOSC) implementation in Lithuania, under the Lithuanian Research Council (LMT). Part of a broader workspace (`lmt_eosc/`) that includes `lt_infra_graph` (infrastructure knowledge graph) and `combined_snapshots` (EOSC framework documentation).

## Data Resources

`resources/` contains survey response data (Excel) from Lithuanian institutions on research data management practices. Content is in Lithuanian.
